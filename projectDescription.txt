This is a webapplication that lets the client add dj-events and concerts, with information of where and when. 
The project uses NextJs and React as frontend technologies, and strapi as CMS and backend.
It is a course based project where I followed this lesson: https://www.udemy.com/share/104pM63@LlnrUEmMSRgFNuEATZB2EdI43mtQzMGW97Bm6H9mUXFk-qaGaZwlqYJwTNbMSA-gcA==/.
